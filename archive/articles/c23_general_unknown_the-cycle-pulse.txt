CITY ENTERS HIGH-DENSITY MODE AS NIGHTS FILL, EVENTS STACK

Cycles 23–33 brought a noticeable rise in city movement: dense foot traffic near Uptown, increased transit use, and a surge in evening gatherings. The Winter Riverfront Festival carried through three cycles, drawing thousands to the waterfront with no disruption to municipal services. Cycle 28 logged a transformer hiccup near Adams Point, creating a brief power flicker but no outage. The city reassured residents the grid remains stable. Cycle 29 included a public-comment session at City Hall, drawing quiet but engaged turnout.
