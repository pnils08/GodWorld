BENJI DILLON — The Golden Arm
P Slayer: What’s the first quiet thought before you throw the first pitch?
Dillon: “Find the seam. Find the breath. Don’t chase perfect—respect chaos.”

Mindset: Counts backwards from five while tracing the two‑seam with a thumb.
Ritual: Reads one page of Science Monthly in the trainer’s room; closes it mid‑sentence for luck.
Stat‑meets‑soul: Opponents hit under two‑hundred after his first page ritual nights.
