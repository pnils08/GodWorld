THE ARTS DURING QUIET MONTHS: A SLOW BUT STEADY PULSE

With no major disruptions, local galleries and rehearsal spaces ran at predictable pace. Musicians booked regular sets, muralists continued long-term pieces, and First Friday planning saw no logistical complications. Quiet cycles tend to strengthen the arts ecosystem by keeping schedules stable.
