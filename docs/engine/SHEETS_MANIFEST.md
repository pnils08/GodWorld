# GodWorld Sheets Manifest

**Last Updated:** 2026-07-20

This manifest is the registry of all active Google Sheets tabs hooked into the Phase 1–11 engine loop, verified against `ENGINE_COUPLING_MAP.md` and `schemas/SCHEMA_HEADERS.md`.

*Note: For the exact column headers of each sheet, reference `schemas/SCHEMA_HEADERS.md` or `SIMULATION_LEDGER_COL_MAP.md`.*

---

## 1. Core State & Demographics
- **`Simulation_Ledger`**: The master citizen substrate.
- **`World_Population`**: Macro-level population and economic metrics.
- **`Neighborhood_Map`**: Spatial definitions and mapping coordinates.
- **`Neighborhood_Demographics`**: Demographic breakdown per neighborhood.
- **`City_Demographics`**: City-wide demographic metrics.
- **`GodWorld_Neighborhoods`**: Base stat blocks for neighborhoods.
- **`GodWorld_Config`**: Global constants and threshold rules.
- **`Simulation_Calendar`**: Cycle-to-date mappings and holidays.

## 2. Infrastructure & Operations
- **`Business_Ledger`**: Tracking businesses, employee counts, and revenue.
- **`Employment_Roster`**: Citizen-to-Business mapping.
- **`Economic_Parameters`**: Reference limits for career and income generation.
- **`Crime_Metrics`**: Safety, property/violent crime indexes per neighborhood.
- **`Transit_Metrics`**: System congestion and delay data.
- **`Chaos_Cars`**: Random disruption logic injection mapping.
- **`Texture_Trigger_Log`**: Environmental texture tracking.

## 3. Civic & Government
- **`Civic_Office_Ledger`**: The 7 active civic offices, holders, and election rules.
- **`Election_Log`**: Historical tracker for election outcomes and margins.
- **`Initiative_Tracker`**: City policy, budgeting, and phase implementation.
- **`Civic_Sweep_Report`**: Macro tracking of the civic sector's health.

## 4. Citizens & Communities
- **`LifeHistory_Log`**: The append-only historical log of all citizen events.
- **`Relationship_Bonds`**: The network graph of citizen relationships.
- **`Household_Ledger`**: Core household tracking (rent, members, income).
- **`Family_Relationships`**: Deep familial bond definitions (marriage, children).
- **`Faith_Ledger`**: Events generated by faith communities.
- **`Faith_Organizations`**: Static definitions of the 16 faith centers.
- **`Community_Programs`**: Non-profit and civic organizations.
- **`Cultural_Ledger`**: Famous entities and recurring culture touchstones.
- **`Generic_Citizens`**: Pre-generated background NPC pool.

## 5. Intake & Generation
- **`Intake`**: The entry point for creating new citizens.
- **`Advancement_Intake1`**: Pre-stage for citizen tier advancement.
- **`Citizen_Media_Usage`**: Media consumption profiling.
- **`Citizen_Usage_Intake`**: Routing usage back into the engine.
- **`Event_Content_Ledger`**: Sheet-resident Phase 5 pool lines + `$SLOT` fragments (S289 Design A). Fail-closed conditions DSL + source whitelist in `loadEventContentLedger.js`; empty tab falls back to hardcoded pools. Writers: hand + post-cycle `scripts/draftContentRows.js` (`auth:auto`). Schema: Kind/PoolKey/Slot/Text/Weight/Conditions/Tags/Grain/Active.

## 6. Narrative & Storytelling
- **`Event_Arc_Ledger`** / **`Arc_Ledger`**: Multi-cycle storyline tension tracking.
- **`WorldEvents_Ledger`** / **`WorldEvents_V3_Ledger`**: Major global events (e.g. weather, crises).
- **`Ripple_Ledger`**: Connecting micro-events to macro-economic shifts.
- **`Event_Ledger`**: Flat list of world events.
- **`Domain_Tracker`**: Evaluating which themes (CIVIC, CULTURE) dominated the cycle.
- **`Health_Cause_Queue`**: Processing severe citizen injuries/illnesses.
- **`Youth_Events`**: Youth-specific trajectory markers.
- **`Cycle_History`**: Payload backups for inter-cycle data bridging.
- **`Cycle_Seeds`**: Base cycle generation starting parameters.

## 7. Media & Newsroom
- **`Media_Ledger`**: Tracking media articles published.
- **`Media_Intake`**: Parsing published articles back into the simulation.
- **`Media_Briefing`**: Pre-publish directives to the LLMs.
- **`Storyline_Tracker`**: Manual storylines designated for media coverage.
- **`Storyline_Intake`**: Parsing media coverage into storyline progress.
- **`Story_Seed_Deck`**: Queued ideas for LLM articles.
- **`Story_Hook_Deck`**: Curated hooks sent to the V3 generator.
- **`Press_Drafts`**: Staging area for article generation.
- **`Cycle_Packet`**: The comprehensive cycle JSON digest.

## 8. Sports & Satellites
- **`Oakland_Sports_Feed`**: Interactive sports event intake.
- **`NBA_Game_Intake`**: Basketball stats and outcomes.
- **`MLB_Game_Intake`**: Baseball stats and outcomes.
- **`Chicago_Feed`**: Satellite world state reference.
- **`Chicago_Sports_Feed`**: Satellite sports reference.
- **`Chicago_Citizens`**: Satellite citizen roster.
