NATIONAL DESK — Dana Reeve

WHY OAKLAND’S CALM WON’T MAKE NATIONAL NEWS (AGAIN)

Eleven cycles of stability rarely register beyond city limits. But consistency matters more than spectacle. Oakland is having a long stretch of both — and it’s a story the country won’t notice until much later, when trends are studied and cities seek models that worked.
