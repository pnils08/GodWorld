Bubic’s command of the sinker-changeup mix and quiet composure made him a steady influence in
clubhouses across Kansas City, Oakland, San Diego, Pittsburgh, Toronto, and Miami. He retired in
2040 with 161 wins, 2 128 strikeouts, and over 2 600 innings pitched, leaving behind a record of
