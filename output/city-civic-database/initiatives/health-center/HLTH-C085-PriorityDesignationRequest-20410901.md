# Resubmission — Priority Municipal Project Designation, Temescal Community Health Center

**CITY OF OAKLAND**
**OFFICE OF CITY PLANNING**

---

**TO:** Office of the Mayor
**FROM:** Mara Vance, City Planning Director
**DATE:** August 2041
**RE:** Resubmission — Priority Municipal Project Designation, Temescal Community Health Center

---

This office recommended priority municipal project designation for the Temescal Community Health Center on the date of its 6-2 council passage. That recommendation has not received a response.

The Health Center remains in design and permitting. Priority designation would compress the permitting timeline by directing city departments to expedite review of project applications. Without designation, the project follows standard review sequencing, which places it behind projects with earlier filing dates regardless of council priority.

The council approved this project with a strong majority. The community health infrastructure it provides is referenced in both the OARI implementation plan and the Stabilization Fund outreach framework as a future service anchor for West Oakland and Temescal.

I am resubmitting the recommendation and requesting a response by September 1, 2041.
