# Request for Construction Mobilization Timeline — Baylight District

**CITY OF OAKLAND**
**OFFICE OF CITY PLANNING**

---

**TO:** Keisha Ramos, Director, Baylight Redevelopment Authority
**FROM:** Mara Vance, City Planning Director
**DATE:** August 2041
**RE:** Request for Construction Mobilization Timeline — Baylight District

---

Director Ramos,

Ordinance BD-83-TIF and Instrument BD-83-REB were filed with your office on Friday. The construction mobilization condition attached to the council's August 12 authorization is satisfied.

The Planning Office has not received a construction mobilization timeline, permit calendar, or milestone schedule from the Redevelopment Authority. These documents are necessary for infrastructure coordination across city departments, including Public Works, the Office of Environmental Review and Sustainability, and AC Transit integration planning.

Please provide the following by September 15, 2041:

1. Projected construction mobilization start date
2. Permit application schedule for Phase I site work
3. Remediation sequencing and estimated duration
4. Milestone schedule through first vertical construction
5. Updated site survey and parcel boundary confirmation for the full 65-acre redevelopment boundary, including current use status of all parcels within the approved district footprint

Item 5 is standard pre-mobilization procedure. The Planning Office requires a current parcel inventory to coordinate infrastructure routing, utility easements, and construction staging across the full district. Any site preparation activity, grading, or survey work occurring on parcels within the boundary should be documented in the response.

The Planning Office is available to coordinate on infrastructure integration at your convenience.
