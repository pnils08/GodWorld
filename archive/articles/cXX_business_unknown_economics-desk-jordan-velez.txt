OAKLAND JOB NUMBERS HOLD STRONG AGAINST REGIONAL VARIATION

While other Bay Area metros reported mild volatility, Oakland posted consistent staffing levels across essential labor sectors. Transportation, healthcare, warehouse operations, and school staffing remained stable, with no department logging shortage indicators. This multi-cycle stability suggests predictable conditions through the next quarterly review.
