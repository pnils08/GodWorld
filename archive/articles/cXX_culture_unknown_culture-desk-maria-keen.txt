THE NEIGHBORHOOD BEAT: EAST OAKLAND IN A LONG QUIET STRETCH

Twelve cycles of calm left East Oakland with something it often lacks: breathing room. Families settled into familiar rhythms — school mornings, buses on time, corner-store chatter returning to a steady hum. No major neighborhood disruptions. Just the everyday life that holds communities together.
