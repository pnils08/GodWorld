**RE:** Stabilization Fund — Disbursement Confirmation Request

---

The OEWD status report transmitted to this office confirmed 47 approved applications with first-round disbursements of approximately $4.2 million projected within three weeks of the report date. That window has closed or is closing.

The Planning Office is tracking initiative implementation timelines across all active civic projects. Please confirm the following:

1. Number of first-round checks delivered to date
2. Number of first-round checks still pending delivery
3. Whether the second disbursement round (projected 50-70 additional approvals, $5-7 million) remains on the six-week timeline
4. Current status of the 154 unreviewed applications against the end-of-October completion target

Individual applicants have contacted this office and the Bay Tribune asking for status updates. The process for notifying applicants of their individual determination status should be confirmed.
