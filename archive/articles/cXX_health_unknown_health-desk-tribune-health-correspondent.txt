MEDICAL LOG: ROUTINE CYCLE, NO SYSTEM FLAGS

Hospitals, clinics, and urgent care centers across the city report volumes consistent with monthly averages. Escalated cases remained within expected limits. Staff turnover for the week was minimal, and there were no shortages or facility alerts.

Doctors describe the cycle as “quiet,” meaning stable — nothing extraordinary, nothing unusual, just the system working as designed.
